How it works

Linglear overlays smart bilingual subtitles on top of your streaming service. Pause, hover and click to explore every word and sentence. Export new words into your preferred spaced repetition system.

Hover translations

Get instant translations and pronunciations by hovering over any word or phrase.

Sentence explanations

Understand grammar and nuance with context-aware sentence breakdowns.

SRS exports

Collect vocabulary as you watch and export it seamlessly to your study deck.
