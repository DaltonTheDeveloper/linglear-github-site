Quick setup

Follow these steps to get Linglear running on your shows.

1) Add to Chrome

Open the Chrome Web Store, search “Linglear”, and click Add to Chrome. Pin the icon for quick access.

2) Open a Show

Visit a supported streaming page. Turn on subtitles, then click the Linglear icon to activate Smart Subtitles.

3) Upgrade Anytime

Need pro features? Use our Stripe-verified checkout and manage your plan in the hosted billing portal.

What you can do

Hover definitions

Point at any word to see meaning, part of speech, and examples. Keep playback flowing — no dictionary rabbit holes.

Sentence translations

Toggle a clean translation under the original line. Switch it off once you understand — back to immersion.

Save to study

One click turns a subtitle line into a review card. We keep your timing and formatting intact for context.

Practice anywhere

Cards work offline and are organized by show/episode so your learning mirrors what you watch.
