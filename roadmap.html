Our Roadmap

We’re building Linglear in the open. Here’s a glimpse at what we've accomplished so far and what's coming next. Timelines are estimates and subject to change as we continually refine the product based on your feedback.

Q4 2024

Beta Launch

The first invite‑only beta rolled out to a small group of polyglots. We validated the core subtitle overlay and vocabulary collection mechanics and gathered invaluable feedback.

Q2 2025

Public Extension Release

Linglear became publicly available as a Chrome extension, allowing anyone to enhance their language learning on supported video platforms. Account creation and the members dashboard were introduced.

Mid 2026

Mobile & Additional Languages

We will expand the extension to Firefox and release a native iOS/Android companion app. More languages and dialects are planned based on community demand.

Beyond 2027

AI Tutor & Personalised Courses

Looking ahead, we aim to integrate an AI tutor that adapts to your learning style and curate personalised courses using your saved vocabulary and viewing habits.
